About Me

I'm Halipuddin, a passionate Frontend Web Developer, Data Engineer, and Coding Teacher based in Makassar, Indonesia. At the age of 23, I ignited my interest in computers and technology by building my first website, and since then, I've wholeheartedly embraced this journey. With a degree in Education, I initially ventured into freelancing as a designer, where I crafted graphics, video content, and websites for small businesses using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Over the years, my focus has evolved towards programming, and I've developed a robust frontend stack, engaging in the creation of innovative projects that address real-world challenges. In addition to my technical pursuits, I have a fulfilling role as a coding teacher, instructing both domestic and international students aged 5-16 in various programming languages, including Scratch, Thunkable, Code.org, Python, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Tinkercad, C++, Java, AR/VR. ->

I guide students in project-based learning, encouraging creativity and skill development. I also teach the fascinating process of game development using Roblox Studio. Committed to continuous personal development, I actively participate in training programs focused on programming and design, ensuring that I stay updated with the latest industry trends. It's my belief that formal degrees are not essential for success in the fields of design or development, as I personally started with no computer science or design degree. Hailing from a non-technical background, I overcame my fear of coding, demonstrating that dedication and passion can lead anyone to excel in these dynamic fields. Beyond coding and development, I also excel in effectively communicating students' progress to their parents, fostering a collaborative learning environment.
